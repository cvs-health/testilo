Introduction

This is a digest of a report of results from a battery of web accessibility tests.

Nine different tools (Alfa, ASLint, Axe, Equal Access, HTML CodeSniffer, Nu Html Checker, QualWeb, Testaro, and WAVE) tested the web page of __org__ at __url__ to check its compliance with various rules. There were a total of about 960 rules, classified into about 290 accessibility issues.

The results were interpreted to yield an aggregate score of __total__, where 0 would be perfect.
