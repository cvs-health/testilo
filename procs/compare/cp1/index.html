The Testaro procedure performs about 600 tests on each page. Of these, 16 tests are custom tests defined by Testaro, and the others belong to packages of tests created by others.

Tests and scoring formulae are fallible and subjective. The reported faults merit investigation as potential opportunities for improved accessibility. But some may not actually harm accessibility, and some other accessibility faults may have escaped detection. Different reasonable procedures could yield different test results and different scores. Testaro and Testilo can be customized to fit different definitions and weightings of types of accessibility.
