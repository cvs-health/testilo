Introduction

The table below compares 1 web pages on accessibility. The page names link to the pages on the web. The scores link to digests that explain in detail how the scores were computed.

The pages were:

Tested by Testaro with procedure tp10

Scored by Testilo with procedure sp10a

Digested by Testilo with procedure dp10a

Compared by Testilo with procedure cp0

The tp10 procedure performs 808 tests on each page. Of these, 16 tests are custom tests defined by Testaro, and the others belong to packages of tests created by others.

Tests and scoring formulae are fallible and subjective. The reported faults merit investigation as potential opportunities for improved accessibility. But some may not actually harm accessibility, and some other accessibility faults may have escaped detection. Different reasonable procedures could yield different test results and different scores. Testaro and Testilo can be customized to fit different definitions and weightings of types of accessibility.
