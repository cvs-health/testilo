Disclaimer: Even though there are widely accepted accessibility standards, there is still no unanimity about how to define, test for, and quantify accessibility. The failures reported below merit investigation as potential opportunities for improved accessibility. But you may conclude that some of the failures do not actually harm accessibility, and some substantial accessibility faults can escape detection by any of these tests. Likewise, you may not agree with weightings and formulas of scoring procedure sp10b, or even question whether it is appropriate to assign accessibility scores to a web page. This digest describes only one interpretation of the page’s accessibility.
