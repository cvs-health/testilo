You can use this report to investigate apparent discrepancies among the tools. On the basis of what you discover, you may draw conclusions such as these:

  • A test of a tool is relatively unreliable.
  • A test of a tool is classified in an incorrect issue.

  • Although one test finds more instances than another, it is still practical to classify them in the same issue.
